About Me.

Hi there! I'm Ryan Wilbert, a student at Michigan State University majoring in Information Science and minoring in Economics. I am a passionate artist and researcher interested in how we can best use technology to handle the spread of information in society. I am most excited by solving accessibility issues and placing a human centered approach to technology. Currently, I am helping with research and promotional content at the SPARTIE Lab, volunteering at my campus' radio station, Impact 89FM, and helping run the Information Science Club.

I come from Lake Orion, Michigan, and I have lived in the state for my whole life. The Midwest has influenced most of it—from my music taste to my general personality. I'm a multifaceted artist, interested in music and audio production, film, and literature. I consider myself a hard working person, and with a quick browse of my work, I hope you can conclude the same.
